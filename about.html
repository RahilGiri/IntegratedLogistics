Milestones

Our Journey

2005 - The Beginning

Integrated Logistics was founded in Hissar with a single booking office and a vision to bridge the logistics gap in North India.

2010 - Western Expansion

Expanded operations to Gujarat and Maharashtra, securing a foothold in major industrial corridors like Vapi and Surat.

2015 - The Mega Hub

Opened a state-of-the-art Transshipment Centre in Ankleshwar, drastically reducing transit times across the western belt.

Today - PAN India

Currently serving 25+ cities PAN-India with a dedicated controlling office in Delhi and a robust fleet of owned and leased vehicles.
